Covid-19

What is Covid-19?

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus.

Coronaviruses are a group of related RNA viruses that cause diseases in mammals and birds. In humans and birds, they cause respiratory tract infections that can range from mild to lethal.

Some preventive measures that were used:

Wash your hands regularly with soap and water, or clean them with alcohol-based hand rub.

Maintain at least 2 meters distance between you and people coughing or sneezing.

Avoid touching your face.

Cover your mouth and nose when coughing or sneezing.

Stay home if you feel unwell.

Refrain from smoking and other activities that weaken the lungs.

Practice physical distancing by avoiding unnecessary travel and staying away from large groups of people.

Symptoms:

Fever.

Dry cough

Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath.

Chest pain or Pressure.

Loss of speech or movement.

Loss of tase or smell.

Sore throat.

Death toll:

Worldwide record indicate that approximately 2,550,000 have died out of 115,000,000 recorded cases and 64,900,000 have recovered.
